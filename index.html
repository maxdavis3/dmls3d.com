DMLS Expertise & Services

01

DMLS Process Mastery

Fiber laser at 200-400μm spot size, 10-20 m/s scan speed, argon atmosphere under 10 ppm O₂. Parameter optimization maximizes density for each alloy system.

02

Six-Alloy Qualification

Six DMLS-qualified alloys with documented process parameters. AlSi10Mg for lightweight structures; 17-4 PH, 316L, Ti6Al4V, Inconel 718, and CoCr for demanding applications.

03

DMLS Post-Processing

Stress relief (650°C-950°C alloy-specific), HIP (2,000°F + 15,000 psi), support removal, heat treat, CNC finish, and CMM inspection — complete DMLS supply chain.

DMLS Technical Depth

Surface-level DMLS knowledge is common. Deep process knowledge — powder rheology, laser-matter interaction, thermal cycling — is our differentiator.

  • ● Powder: 40-100μm, pre-alloyed for consistency
  • ● Chamber: argon <10 ppm O₂ inert atmosphere
  • ● Laser: 200-500W fiber, tuned per alloy
  • ● Layers: 20-60μm per scan pass
  • ● Build rate: 5-20 cm³/hour alloy-dependent
  • ● Post-HIP: closes residual porosity to zero
